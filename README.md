Road Accident Analysis Dashboard
This repository features a comprehensive Road Accident Dashboard developed in MS-Excel. The project provides a data-driven overview of road casualties for the year 2022, comparing them against 2021 data to identify trends, high-risk conditions, and vehicle-specific insights.
________________________________________
<br> Key Performance Indicators (2022)
•	Fatal Casualties: 2,855 
•	Serious Casualties: 27,045 
•	Slight Casualties: 1,658 
•	Total Car Casualties: 15,580 <\br>
________________________________________
Visual Insights & Analysis
1. Casualties by Light and Location
The data reveals a significant disparity between urban and rural environments:
•	Urban Areas: Account for the majority of casualties at 121.3K.
•	Rural Areas: Account for 74.5K casualties.
•	The dashboard segments these by Daylight and Dark conditions to identify visibility-related risks.
2. Road Type and Surface Impact
Infrastructure plays a critical role in accident frequency:
•	Single Carriageway: The highest risk road type with 144.7K casualties.
•	Dual Carriageway: 31.9K casualties.
•	Roundabout: 12.7K casualties.
•	One-way Street: 3.5K casualties.
•	Slip Road: 2.2K casualties.
3. Temporal Trends (2021 vs. 2022)
The project includes a month-by-month comparison of Current Year (CY) vs. Previous Year (PY) casualties:
•	2021 Totals: Urban (144.5K) and Rural (51.2K).
•	Monthly Tracking: Data is visualized from January through December to identify seasonal spikes.
